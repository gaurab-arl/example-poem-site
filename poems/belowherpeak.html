Far Below Her Peak

Far away from life hearts rest on a bench one white as snow, a Shade my eyes beheld. She was the mountain, Ranking those closest to her peak. I was the one at the far bottom, Looking up at the moon-like princess, Concealing herself from me. Casting a pity-filled gaze my way. I, a mere mortal, Had my essence drained away. Not willing to keep fragile me, The seed of a grown, otherworldly being.
- Gaurab Aryal
