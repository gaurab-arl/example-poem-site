Fear

Fear Inside us Made us feel Dead. The funny things to remember Are slowly disappearing. The moments we spent Are scattering. The peace we once loved Is fading. The symbol of our existence Is decreasing. The friend I never could be In this lifetime — Is a pure dream. I send your way My undying will. Three reasons as a person Still keep us existing. But the problems We once solved Are increasing. In science, the Three-Body Problem Is never Solved — And neither will mine Be solved In my existence. The fear we face Is just a wall We have to overcome. Fear, panic, other things — Disappear With a hug. The cold stare we dream of Others giving us — Is the fear We’ve built inside. I feel it. Someone to share A burden with — That is love. That is friendship. That is what every bond means. A special person in life — We all need one. The redness we feel — A warm feeling. The pain burning. The coldness we get. The death we feel Inside — The undead thing We seems.
- Gaurab Aryal
