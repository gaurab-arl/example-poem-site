Who are you ?

Who Are You Like water showing my face, Each time I stir, It shows different. I can't seem to understand— You didn't seem to tell me, As I want to know more about you. You seem distant from me. A smiley face, Each time different, In a quiet place, Talking with my Inner self, Convincing me to Sleep away. Yesterday was different, So today. Sometimes we are together, Many times we are far away. A person can't seem To stay in your head— A sleepy mind, With a sleepy face. Once a single body With two souls— Now I seem to see it: You tried to separate. Just showing my way, Maybe hiding The fact that you are Together in a loving phase.
- Gaurab Aryal
