Day With You In My Grave

A peaceful day — with you in my grave, You in my arms, and I in yours. In this accursed world, only you cared. The rest — like wolves, starved for years. This year, something struck me hard, Distorted the way I used to think. A temporary gem I once held dear — Now, with time… I kept it for years, only to throw it away. In this vast universe, I am but an ant, So small, so easy to erase. I only wanted to admire its beauty — Without makeup, just… nature and bare. Worried about everything — except me. But I already got the answer, Even if you never shared it. We search for someone shaped in our minds — You found yours——I lost mine. Still, I’m just an ant. Existing only through nature’s belief. Don’t look at my path — I follow, I swallow. A road into the void I walk, Searching for something… But in the end, We both lie still, in each other’s arms — Just locked away, With the memories we shared in my grave.
- Gaurab Aryal
