Beauty

The beauty I see — It’s not what I wanted. I just want to appreciate it. Makeup or natural, I accept it. All of you — I have seen It all. The beauty you hide Inside — I saw your inner self. I wrote and sent it... But I never got an answer. Don’t worry, I am here, Without knowing myself. Existing only through The belief Of seeing you. But— The way you look Feels forced. It’s like A road you don’t want to go down, But it’s the only one Available for you.
- Gaurab Aryal
