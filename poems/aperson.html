A Person Beside

A Person Beside A man or woman — You decide. I can't see men, Just take the woman’s side. It's just I can't Look at you with Other men by side. Like rain falling From the sky, It will rain on me Even if I try to hide. But It will burn me from inside, Even if it falls from sky, Touching my skin. It feels like acid, Going deeper and deeper With every drop on me. It seems it can't flood me — It wants me to become Hollow. Like an empty shell Without a soul Present inside. How beautiful is the sky, But it can't seem As beautiful as you are. You look like sunshine — Bright and dark Side by side. But I only seem to be In dark. Who is on the bright side? Maybe it's your friend Whom you talk to freely — Not with me, Being forced to. A fake smile By my side. Not even a bit funny, I seem To identify. But a genuine smile By your person’s side — Don’t know what it feels like. I seem to be trapped By an annoying side, With a smile fading Like a waterfall Going dry.
- Gaurab Aryal
