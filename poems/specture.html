The Departure of My Yearning

The departure of my yearning, a shield for the misdeeds of evil. A wounded being wanted to heal, God’s sign listening to my story. Shielding tears for lies, A bucket filled for drowning. Memories return as phantoms, Each breath an act of survival. Specters beneath the moon, I kept searching for resonance, Winds of pity-pierced through me, Unfolding every meaning behind it.
- Gaurab Aryal
