A Room of Six, and Six Stories Untold A room filled with ----- people, Each with their own ambition. Filled with hope, A room filled with love, Bonded by relationship— A love bounded by friendship. A first person———— Filled with hope to be the best, Attracted to a person— Unexpected. Changed in some ways I can't describe, Gets to a person's side, I suspect. Lost in a train Going in the opposite direction From reaching A destined place. A second person———— A protective brother type, Loved a childish side. A man who wanted her to live. A person To support her in need, Even when All others hide. Almost a father, A child she had In her mind, A person she wanted to be with, A person she wanted to love. A third person————— A strong woman, flows in emotions Like no one. Afraid of loving—and to be loved. A smile hiding real pain inside. A head shifted toward a person on the side, An unstable man, but with a loving side. A feeling hidden I can't see, The person I can't reach. She seems to be searching— A person by my side. He seems like a funny friend. A fourth person————— An unstable person Wanting to explore The world. Thinking alone, Don’t know what made him love. Once faced a fallback, Always trying to get up. Now I don’t think That was false. A flower leaning to his side, Afraid to be seen— A private love story (maybe). A fifth person————— A childish person, Didn’t know anything existed. A forgetful mind with A negligent kind, Looking for unexpected reasoning. A favorite person of all, Just hiding truth, Never wanting to feel Sad. A daybreak, A moment, A silence that follows Whenever she goes. A sixth person———— An innocent child, Always disturbing Whenever I’m in sight. A broken person— Well, fault was his. Climbed a tall mountain And Jumped without seeing. "Just leave me alone for a bit..." I can't say anything to you, As you are related to a person I dream.
