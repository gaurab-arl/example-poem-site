Ma Jasto

Mero mutu lageu timile, malai darkauna na deu. Timi jata chhau, malai herna deu. Timro khushi ma malai ramauna deu. Timro saath na huda pani, "Timro saathi ho," bhan na deu. Sanjhako bela haami hunthiyau bhet— bokera aafno maan ko kura. Aafno mann ma pida heriraheko thiyau— ek arkā lai anubhav janthiyau, bujhera ek arkā ko pida. Sundar timro muhar, hira jastai timra aakha— mandirma rakheko murti jasto: jati herda ni heri rahun jasto. Sanga haali rakheko chu— mann ma dherai kura. Kasari bhanu timilai sāra? Bujdainaun bhancha timi— khoi, kasto kasto? Dherai kura lukaune bhaeko chhau, ma jasto.
- Gaurab Aryal
